Places to Visit in Uttarakhand

Kedarnath

Kedarnath is a holy town located in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand.

Mussoorie

Mussoorie is a popular hill station known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate.

Nainital

Nainital is a charming hill station situated around the Naini Lake.

Badrinath

Badrinath is one of the holiest towns for Hindus, located in the Chamoli district.
